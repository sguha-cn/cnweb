Overview

Simply stated, user engagement is about connecting with more customers, more frequently, more personally, through more channels, in a more meaningful way. You need to engage with your customers based on where, when, and how they want to engage i.e. whether they’re playing a game on their iphones, looking for the answer to a question on their iPads, or sharing their interests and likes on Facebook and Twitter. A right engagement strategy targets them where they are and using the right message.

Our User Engagement Services Include:

Landing Page Design

The purpose of a landing page is to encourage and pursue the visitors to take a single well defined action on your website. This can be filling out a form, signing up for a trial or downloading some content. A typical engagement might look something like this.

  • Stranger goes to Google and performs a search
  • Stranger clicks on a link to your blog article or your sponsored ad and becomes a visitor
  • Visitor is redirected to your landing page and gets specific, more detailed information about your offering
  • Visitor completes the form on your landing page
  • Visitor becomes a new lead
  • Lead is redirected to Thank You Page (You sell more or higher value services to the prospect)

Remarketing

Remarketing can help you reach people who have previously visited your website but haven’t made exited without taking any action. Using services such as Google Adwords, Facebook and AdRoll, you can target visitors specifically and anonymously based on the pages that they have visited and then target them again. Here is what we can help you with:

  • Tagging and setup: We will create the tags for the lists and the remarketing campaigns. The people who visits your product web pages will be automatically added to the list that we will target with the remarketing ads.
  • Creative / banner creation. We will create multiple banner ads for your remarketing campaign, and keep testing different creative throughout the time, in order to get optimal results. In case you already have your own banner ads we can use them, but be aware that creative for remarketing campaigns needs to meet specific sizes and format
  • Ongoing management. We will closely monitor your campaign and keep making improvements and adjustments to get the best possible ROI.

Microsites

Microsites are small websites that typically consist of 3-6 pages and are used to supplement the information on your primary website. They promote a specific service or product while remaining separate from other products or brands. Microsites are great for promoting special offers, or conducting contests and sweepstakes. They offer an additional benefits as they tend to fly under the radar of competitors and saturate the search engine results for given product or service.

Our web services team can develop fully customized and branded websites and microsites. These websites can either be stand alone or embedded into your existing website. All of the microsites we build are done using WordPress as a CMS, ensuring that performance never drops off. It also means that once your site is set up, you will be able to log in and make any edits or updates to the content that you require.

Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing, or SMM, is a form of internet marketing that implements various social media networks in order to achieve marketing communication and branding goals. Social media marketing primarily covers activities involving social sharing of content, videos, and images for marketing purposes:

A) Social media audit: This is an in-depth analysis of your site’s presence across the various social networks and a strategy for ways to improve it. Not every site has a place on Digg or MySpace, so we cater our suggestions to realistic options including niche opportunities and relevant mainstream tactics.

B) Social profile creation: We will identify the social networks where you should be participating and where people are talking about you. We’ll also help you create and manage your own social media profiles to attract visitors and leverage the two-way conversation.

C) Blog design, setup and/or optimization: A blog that is not setup correctly won’t attract readers or bring you the search engine benefits you’re after. We’ll work with you to find the best blogging platform for your needs, help you to optimize your blog for success from the very beginning, and show you how to tweak it to make it more accessible to both users and the search engines.

D) Social Media Management – We will ensure your regular and fresh content is posted on your Social Media profile.

Benefits

Become more trustworthy

People feel re-assured by the presence of a regularly updated Twitter feed on a website (hours, not days). It shows professionalism, authority and clearly displays that you are “open for business”. You will see an increase in your website conversion rates.

Return on investment

You’ll never see a better return on investment than a well run social media campaign. Google is now returning social media results in amongst it’s search results and Twitter and Facebook have literally hundreds of millions of users. You can get access to this market for a tiny monthly investment when compared to other marketing channels.

Get ahead of your competitors

Most of your competitors will probably not be doing a good job of embracing social media, so this is your chance to race ahead. If they are engaging well in the social networks then you must get involved or you risk being left behind and losing your audience to your competition. Using social media properly is a great opportunity to get ahead of your competition.

Respond to harmful criticism

People talk about your business online—are you answering their questions, concerns and criticisms? There is nothing worse than not answering to comments or questions that are posted for you on Social media.

Improve your authority

When people see you have a busy social network with hints and trivia relating to your chosen business field they will automatically associate authority with it. Your business will be seen as having increased credibility and specialist knowledge which, in turn, will also increase your sales conversion rates.
