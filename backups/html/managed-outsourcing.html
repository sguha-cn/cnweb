Overview

We engage specialist, smart teams for our clients who immerse themselves in clients business and help them focus more on their business objectives rather than worrying about or doing technical stuff. For all intents and purposes we function as your extended team working from our development centre in India.

Thus using our services, Clients enjoy the Cost benefits and Flexibility of hiring through leading freelancing and crowdsourcing portals such as Odesk or, Elance along with the Quality, Reliability and Privacy of working with established consultancies.

Hire Vetted Staff and Not Freelancers

All the staff members whom you hire are our full-time employees and hence they are pre-screened, well trained and vetted. This saves you the time of sifting through resumes and conducting interviews and gives you assurance that you are getting the exact skills and work that you are paying for. Once you are happy with everything and wish to proceed, we put the staff directly in your control.

Get Started Quickly

Based on your specific business needs, our specialist business advisors will make recommendation on the skillsets and number of staff that you need to hire. Starting at just $10/Hr, You will be able to hire remote staff who will be able to work for from Monday to Friday, 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM. We try our best to adjust our working hours to ensure that there is ample overlap regardless of what timezone that you are in.

Communicate Daily or, As Frequently as Your Wish

You are now free to begin interacting with the team as frequently as you want. We provide free access to Teamwork or Basecamp which are our preferred communication tool. However, we are open to recommendations and quite often work within client systems as well. Besides those, you can also communicate via Skype, Webex, VOIP, Google Hangouts etc Your team will have everything that they need to perform the work including Development servers that they can use to test their work.

Every Remote Staff is Physically Supervised

As an additional step to ensure that you get quality and output, we assign a supervisor who will oversee your staff and ensure that all the tasks are delivered on time and as per your requirements. You can decide, whether you want to communicate with the each team member individually or, use your assigned supervisor as a single point of contact. Our advisors are always there to help you better collaborate and work with your offshore staff!

Monitor Your Staff

Hours worked are monitored by online timesheets as well as entries made in project management tool. So you can see exactly what they are working on. Unless needed for your work all non-business websites are filtered out, including Social Networks like Facebook. This ensures that your staff can work without getting distracted and maximizes their productivity. This also provides greater data security as we track all websites visit, FTP uploads and all computer programs that they are running.

Renew Monthly

You receive only one invoice every month for renewal of your staff after 30 calendar days are completed.

We can help your business grow faster, save you up to 70% in costs and greatly improve your time to market. To get started, all you need to do is get in touch with us and tell us about your requirements. Let us know if you want us to sign an NDA and we will be happy to accommodate your request. We respect your privacy and we will never share any details about you or your business to people outside of our organization.
