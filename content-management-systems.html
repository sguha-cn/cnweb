Overview

Capital Numbers’ CMS developers have carried out custom CMS development for clients across various industry verticals fulfilling all their e-commerce and web application administration needs. With our strong expertise in web CMS development, we offer custom CMS software development either from scratch or customizing existing software such as –

1. WordPress
2. Drupal
3. Joomla
4. Magento

Here is what we can do for you:

Custom Theme Development

CMS system offers you the access and flexibility of updating your website content and graphics yourself. We hand code each custom theme to the specifications of the website design. Thus, your website does not need to compromise on how it looks and yet remains easy to manage.

PSD to Theme Conversion

Our team of CMS experts can turn your design into a fully functional theme that is ready for you to use. Everything from the header to the footer work exactly as you’d envisioned. With Capital Numbers, you get consistent, precise, and prompt service each and every time. Get started by sending us your design. Our CMS conversion experts will quickly review it to determine if there is any complexity and then confirm back the price quote. We will start working on your theme immediately upon approval of the quote.

Website Migration

Running a HTML based website without a CMS can be very difficult and time consuming. Overtime navigation becomes outdated and it becomes extremely difficult to keep all the information organized properly. We can help migrate your existing website on to a CMS of your choice, so that you can keep your website updated and benefit from all the additional features that it offers.

Website Maintenance

Universal problem – developers do not like to do “maintenance” jobs. Good developers, especially the young ones find stuff as posting new content, checking links etc to be quite boring and they always want to be working on exciting new technologies. There is also a lack of career advancement in maintenance. Thus, hiring and retaining talented developers can be changeling for maintenance roles. Our team of in-house professionals take care of everything for you so that you don't have to worry about hiring and retention.
