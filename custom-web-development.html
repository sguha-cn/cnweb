Overview

At Capital Numbers we always try to find the most efficient, reliable and cost effective solution for our clients.

Sometimes it is extremely difficult to adopt a pre-packed, out of the box solution as per your business needs. At Capital Numbers, we do not believe in reinventing the wheels rather our custom web development solution uses proven methodologies and framework to build websites as per your exact specifications.

All of these tools we use have open licensing, meaning you own your system from top to bottom, with no restrictions on how you can deploy it in the future.

  • No annual licensing fees
  • No royalties or restrictions on resale
  • No restrictions on where you host

Here is what we can do for you:

Web Application Development

We focus on building complex custom web applications that solve complex business problems. We custom develop each web application so you know that it is tailored to your exact business requirements. We deliver functional, data driven web applications combining our solid cross-browser experience, technological expertise and established development methodology.

Web BasedProductDevelopment

We excel in delivering on our promises to our clients, which allows them to go to market faster and better than their competition. We develop products that will meet your requirements, functions better and will make your customers repeat customers. We excel in fast pace and ever changing environments and have extensive experience in commercial product development for small to large companies across the globe.

Software as a Service (SAAS)

We provide custom solutions for new SAAS web application development. Our area of expertise covers many industries and we can create a custom SAAS application that is created just for your unique business requirements.

Maintenance

Our web application maintenance and support services ensure stable and uninterrupted operation of your business system. The services include problems analysis and resolution, upgrades, user interface re-design and 3rd party system integrations.
