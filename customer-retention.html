Overview

Consider the following facts:

  • Acquiring new customers can cost as much as five times more than satisfying and retaining current customers
  • 2% increase in customer retention has the same effect as decreasing costs by 10%
  • Reducing your customer defection rate by 5% can increase your profitability by 25% to 125%
  • Customer profitability tends to increase over the life of a retained customer.

Email Marketing

E-mail marketing can benefit small and large organizations alike. Our E-mail marketing services allow you to affordably test E-mail marketing and either supplement your current advertising campaign with another channel (increasing frequency of your message) or advertise to a potentially untapped community of potential clients or consumers of your message (increasing reach)

Here is what we do for you:

1. Mailing List Import and Setup
We can extract your customer list and setup a proper email list. This list is always maintained up-to-date as we remove all bounced addresses, invalid emails and unsubscribe requests. This ensures that your email list is always clean and has a high reputation rating.

2. Email Creative Services to Pass Spam Filters
If your HTML emails aren’t designed properly, it will be marked as spam by all major email providers like Gmail, Hotmail and Yahoo. Also, a lot of people check their emails on mobile phones and it’s critical that you ensure it’s readable on smaller screens

3. Inbox Delivery Management
There is no point sending out emails unless they can reach the inbox of your clients. ISPs keep updating their spam filters and we will help ensure that your email has high delivery rate.

4. Campaign Setup and Broadcast
You need a proper infrastructure to broadcast emails. We can send your email to your own customer database at an agreed upon monthly rate depending on your volume.

5. Campaign Reporting
Upon completion of every campaign we share reports like how many emails were delivered, how many were opened, how many unsubscribed etc. Any outgoing links can be tagged with Google Analytics code to ensure you can track conversions on your website.

Benefits

Retain existing customers

Here is the basic math – A firm with a 70% customer retention rate will have lost two to three times as many customers as a firm with a 90% retention rate. Thus, the effect of customer retention on the bottom line cannot be underestimated. Our services help you keep in touch with your existing customers and serve them better.

Gain referral

Happy customers tell their friends and business associates about you. By serving your existing clients better you improve your chances of gaining referrals which often tend to long term and very profitable customers.

Up-sell and cross-sell

When you will have all the data about your customers stored and segmented properly, you will know exactly who your customers are and what services have they expressed interest in. You can then systematically target and create opportunites for generating more sales.

Increase customer lifetime value (CLV)

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV or CLTV) is increasingly being recognised as one of the most important measures of the worth of a customer. It takes into account not only the customer's value now but the expected value over their projected lifetime as a customer. It is arguably the best way a marketer can demonstrate unequivocally that a programme is working: the CLV of targeted customers must increase

Create brand advocates

Advocacy is one of the highest forms of loyalty that a customer can show. Advocates are so satisfied and pleased with your offering that they tell their friends and associates. To most people, a personal recommendation is far more convincing than any amount of promotional material they receive - even if they already trust the brand
