Overview

Get the most out of advertising investment, right from the very beginning, by using the experience, support and know-how of Capital Numbers. We are supported and certified by Google Adwords to save you time and money while taking advantage of this highly effective method of advertising.

Right from the very beginning, instead of talking with sales people, you get advice from an experienced, certified and dedicated account manager who will be your single point of contact. Your account manager develops a one to one relationship with you and your business to plan and execute specific campaigns to reach your goals.

Our experienced team possesses valuable knowledge from our years of experience, enabling you to instantly benefit from proven strategies that work. More than that, staying on top of Google Adwords and Bing Ads ever changing platforms, competitors constantly changing bids and ads requires ongoing attention. Our team will make necessary adjustments to the maximise your ROI , keeping in touch with you constantly

Benefits

Certified experts

Capital Numbers is a Google Certified Partner and our certified representatives provide direct support for all of your needs.

Dedicated account managers

Capital Numbers provides each client with a qualified, expert account manager. This account manager will take time to get to know your business, its goals, key differentiators and the best possible way to implement the campaign. All our PPC account managers certified, trained and well versed in PPC management. We all work from under the same roof and available to talk to you via VOIP, Telephone and Email. You can count on us to handle any need that may arise at any time.

Improve your ROI

If you rely on yourself or cookie-cutter PPC management advise to manage your campaign, you'll end up buying *a lot* of clicks that don’t bring you any results. Our experts help you get sustainable traffic from Google by only choosing keywords that are related to your business.

Save time

A Google Adwords campaign takes time to set up – you have to research keywords, write ads and create the campaign settings. But once the campaign is set up, it doesn’t end there - once the campaign is up and running it needs regular monitoring and management.

For example, some ads will work better than others, you’ll need to track which keywords perform best, which devices work best, what times of day or days of the week are best for conversions, which ad extensions work best and which locations are most profitable. And you have to use this information to adjust your bidding strategy and campaign settings to maximise results. All of this takes TIME.

And if you are not an expert, it will take longer. If you or a member of your team is spending just a few hours a week on Adwords then I encourage you to work out the cost of that each month – multiply total time spent by the hourly rate of the individual doing the work. It adds up.
